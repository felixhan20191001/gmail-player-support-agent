We're sorry to hear you're having trouble with ads in the game. We understand how frustrating it is when the game shuts down or your progress is affected whenever an ad appears.

Ad loading or playback problems can be related to the ad itself, your network connection, device performance, or available storage space. Please check that your connection is stable and that your device still has enough free storage. If storage is low, try clearing space by removing apps or files you no longer need.

If you want to keep your current game progress, please do not uninstall and reinstall the game casually, as that may reset local save data.

If the issue continues, please send us a screenshot or screen recording of the ad, along with when it happened and your device model. That will help us investigate and report the problematic ad.
