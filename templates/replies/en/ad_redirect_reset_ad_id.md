We're sorry to hear that ads are interrupting your gameplay, especially when they redirect your phone to external websites or apps. We understand how annoying that can be.

This type of issue is usually related to third-party ad content or your device's advertising identifier, rather than in-game logic. We recommend resetting your device's ad ID to refresh how advertisers recognize your device. This often reduces repeated disruptive ads of the same kind.

**On iOS:**
1. Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
2. Turn off Personalized Ads, wait a few seconds, then turn it back on (or leave it off)
3. Force-quit the game completely, reopen it, and check whether the redirects still happen

**On Android:**
1. Open Settings → Google → Ads (on some devices: Settings → Privacy → Ads)
2. Tap Reset advertising ID
3. Force-quit the game completely, reopen it, and check whether the redirects still happen

If the problem continues after resetting, please reply with when the ad usually appears (for example after completing a level or when claiming a reward) and share a screenshot or screen recording if possible. We'll continue investigating and report the ad to our ad partners.

Thank you for your patience and understanding.
