You can delete the app from your iPhone through the normal iOS app removal flow. Touch and hold the app icon, choose Remove App, then choose Delete App.

Please note that game save progress is stored locally on the device. Deleting the app or clearing its data may reset your current progress, and the previous save may not be recoverable.
